No Installation Required

Game2Learn educational products require no installation. Although for maximum efficiency we do recommend that you copy the program from the CD-ROM onto your desktop. This:

  • Protects the CD from scratching when being continiously loaded and unloaded from your CD drive
  • Will help to improve the overall speed and efficiency of the program
  • Makes it a quick and simple process for a child to open the program. They simply double click on the desktop icon. Further adding to the self-directed learning experience that Game2Learn educational products provide.

Game2Learn educational products are self-contained and can be played on any computer that supports Adobe® players. (Check your computer’s operating system for details)

You may require the latest versions of Adobe® Shockwave® and Flash players® (Available to download from www.adobe.com) to run this CD-ROM.

Tablet versions of Game2Learn educational products are currently under development. If you would like to be notified when they are available please send us an email through the ‘Contact us’ link or watch for them on Google Play®.

Featured Product

Math Foundations

Math Foundations with Q&A is based on early learning principles and designed to foster independent learning skills. Math Foundations with Q&A helps children develop a sound understanding of:

• Grouping
• Shapes
• Measuring
• Telling time
• Ordering

Designed to also support the Foundation Year of the Australian and State Curricula and 21st century digital learning principles, Math Foundations with Q&A includes more than 340 interactive learning objects across the Learn, Play and Check sections.
